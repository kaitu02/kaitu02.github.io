About me

I am currently an Assistant Professor in College of Mathematics and Statistics at Shenzhen University. From 2021 to 2023, I was a postdoc at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University and The Hong Kong University, working with Dr. Man-Chung Yue. In 2020, I received my Ph.D. degree in Operational Research and Cybernetics from Beijing University of Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Haibin Zhang. I obtained my M.S. in Operational Research and Cybernetics under the supervision of Prof. Fuquan Xia in 2016 and B.S. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematic in 2013, both from Sichuan Normal University.

Research Interests

  • My research focuses on continuous optimization and its application in robust optimization problems.
