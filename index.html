About me

I'm currently working at School of Mathematical Science, Sichuan Normal University as a Lecturer. From 2021 to 2022, I was a postdoc at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, working with Dr. Man-Chung Yue. In 2020, I got my Ph.D. in Operational Research and Cybernetics at Beijing University of Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Haibin Zhang. I received my M.S. in Operational Research and Cybernetics under the supervision of Prof. Fuquan Xia in 2016 and B.S. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematic in 2013, both from Sichuan Normal University. Here is my google scholar.

Research Interests

My research focuses on designing efficient methods and developing the corresponding theory analysis for convex/nonconvex optimization problems with special structures, which have wide applications in data analytics, image processing, machine learning, and uncertainty optimization, among others. I am particularly interested in the following topics.

  • Variational inequality problem, Difference-of-convex problem and (Distributionally) Robust Optimization, etc.
